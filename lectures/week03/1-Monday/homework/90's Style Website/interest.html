About Joy's Interest

Dance: Dance is a performing art form consisting of sequences of movement, either improvised or purposefully selected. This movement has aesthetic and often symbolic value. Dance can be categorized and described by its choreography, by its repertoire of movements, or by its historical period or place of origin.





Karaoke: Karaoke is a form of entertainment, offered typically by bars and clubs, in which people take turns to sing popular songs into a microphone over prerecorded backing tracks.





Play Tennis: Tennis is a game played on a level court by two or four players who use rackets to hit a ball back and forth across a low net dividing the court.
